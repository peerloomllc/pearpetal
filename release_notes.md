The big one this release: PearPetal now works across more than one of your devices. Scan a QR code from your phone to link a tablet or a second phone, and your cycle log, your profile and your settings follow you there - still with no account, no server and no cloud. Everything syncs directly between your own devices, encrypted end to end.

New
  
  - Link your devices with a QR code. Open Cycle settings → Your devices to generate a code, scan it from the other device, and you're paired. New devices can link right from the welcome screen. You can see everything you've linked, and remove a device whenever you like.
  - A recovery phrase. PearPetal can now show you a twelve-word phrase that restores your identity and lets you re-link your devices if you lose one. It doesn't restore your cycle log on its own - that's what an encrypted backup is for, so keep making those.
  - Your settings travel with you. Name, avatar, cycle lengths, goal, flower, conditions and birth control now match on every device you've linked, and update as soon as they sync.

Improved
  
  - A calmer Cycle view. The whole view fits on one screen now. Logging a day opens in a sheet that slides up over the dial instead of taking you somewhere else, the dial hints at today, and moving between months cross-slides rather than jumping.
  - Support development, on iPhone too. The About section and the occasional thank-you nudge now appear on iOS the same way they always have on Android. PearPetal is free, and stays free - this just makes the tip jar reachable.

Fixed

  - The About screen shows the right version. It was stuck reading 0.1.0 no matter what you had installed.

Existing logs carry over automatically the first time you open this version. Nothing you've tracked leaves your device.
