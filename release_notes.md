PearPetal is here! A private, peer-to-peer menstrual and fertility cycle tracker. Your data lives only on your own phone, with no account, no server and no cloud.

  - Track your cycle on a living petal dial: a flower that furls and blooms across your phase, with next-period, fertile-window and ovulation estimates computed right on your device
  - Log period start and flow, symptoms, mood, notes and basal body temperature
  - Share a scoped, read-only view with a partner - cycle phase only, your fertile window, or your full cycle - and end it any time

  More in this first release:
  - Goals that change the tone: general tracking, trying to conceive, avoiding, or a dedicated pregnancy mode
  - Predictions adjust for conditions like PCOS, endometriosis or thyroid, and for birth control
  - A month calendar view alongside the dial
  - Optional local reminders for your period or fertile days, with a discreet lock-screen mode
  - Encrypted, password-protected backups to move to a new phone - nothing is ever uploaded
  - Everything is peer-to-peer and end-to-end encrypted, so your cycle never touches a server

  Open source at https://github.com/peerloomllc/pearpetal
