A small maintenance release. Nothing about how you track your cycle has changed - this one is about the app staying responsive when you come back to it.

Fixed

  - Coming back to a frozen screen. On some Android phones, leaving PearPetal in the background for a while and then returning could leave the screen sitting still. Nothing you logged was ever lost or at risk, but you had to close the app and open it again to get moving. Now PearPetal quietly refreshes itself when you come back. After a long time away it may take a second to settle, and then you are right where you left off.

As always, everything you track stays on your own devices.
