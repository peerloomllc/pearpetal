PearPetal 1.0 - the first release.

A private, peer-to-peer menstrual and fertility cycle tracker. Your data lives only on your own phone: no accounts, no servers, no cloud.

 - Track your cycle on a living petal dial, with next-period, fertile-window and ovulation estimates computed right on your device
 - Share a scoped, read-only view with a partner (cycle phase only, your fertile window, or your full cycle) and end it any time
 - Log period, flow, symptoms, mood, notes and BBT; goal-aware for tracking, conceiving, avoiding or pregnancy, and it adjusts for  conditions and birth control

Also in 1.0: a month calendar, optional discreet reminders, password-encrypted backups to move phones, five flowers to choose from, and end-to-end encryptedpeer-to-peer sync so your cycle never touches a server.
